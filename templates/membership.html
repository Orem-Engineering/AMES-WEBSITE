Membership

Types of membership in Association of Mechanical Engineers Students.

  1. Home
  2. Membership

Ordinary Membership

  • This is open to students admitted to the School of Mechanical and Manufacturing Engineering at the Technical University of Kenya.

Alumni Membership

  • This is open to former members of AMES who have completed their undergraduate studies and still support the Club’s in any way. It's also open to any graduate of SMME who has interests in club.

Social Membership:

  • This is open to person's who have with interest in club’s welfare but would only want to participate in activities of interest to them.

Honorary Membership

  • AMES may by a resolution at a duly convened General Meeting, invite and admit as an honorary member, any person who in the opinion of the Club has rendered outstanding.
