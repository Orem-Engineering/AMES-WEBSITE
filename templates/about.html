About Us

AMES is an amazing platform for enthusiastic students to gain industrial exposure and valuable field insight, thanks to industrial visits and career talks organized. Moreover members can also benefit from mentorship sessions,workshops and seminars.AMES was founded in August 2023 by Andrea Lentaaya perez,Opiyo Mung'oni Clayton,Kennedy Kamau Mwangi and Omwa Agumba Isaiah with intension of harmonizing all cades of Mechanical Engineering. AMES aim to to give adequate industry exposture.Our values are

  • Hardwork.
  • Reselient.
  • Patience.
